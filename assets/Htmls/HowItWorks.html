How It Works
1. Select which output for your coins’ value:
-Charity Donation
-Gift Card
-Bank Account
-Octopus Card
2. Read and accept the ‘Terms of Use’/ Some options incur a service fee.
3. Pour in your coins and let the Coin Dragon count them. After that, you can choose to accept/refund your coin count.
4. Check your receipt. Email it or take a photo for reference.
