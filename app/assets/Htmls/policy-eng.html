Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 18th, 2019

Coin Dragon Limited ('Coin Dragon') owns the Coin Dragon brand, including the associated business, website, and other associated activities.  If you have questions or concerns about Coin Dragon's privacy practices, please contact us at info@coindragon.co INFORMATION WE COLLECT

 We collect information about you in various ways, including:
Transaction Information: your output choices, the amount you entered, the time you used the kiosk
Log Information: We observe the timing related to each function/scene of your user interface
CCTV Security Footage
Mobile Numbers (receipt)
Email Addresses (receipt or gift card code delivery)
Bank Account Number
Octopus Card Number

HOW WE USE THE INFORMATION WE COLLECT

 We may use information about you to:
a) Electronically deliver digital receipts
b) Deliver gift card codes electronically
c) Follow up with customers/vendors for faulty transactions
d) Troubleshoot erroneous transactions internally
e) Reconcile transaction amounts with vendors/partners
f) Provide the necessary information to facilitate the transfer of exchanged coins minus fees (ie. Bank Account Number)
g) Improve our customer experience using our kiosk
h) For suspected crime, we may use recorded CCTV to aid police investigations

WHAT WE DO NOT DO WITH YOUR INFORMATION:

 a) WE DO NOT Disclose this information to third parties
b) WE DO NOT Track your previous transactions with Coin Dragon
c) WE DO NOT Use your contact information for marketing purposes

SECURITY OF INFORMATION

 We strive at all times to ensure that your personal data will be public against unauthorised or accidental access, processing, erasure or other use. We maintain this commitment to data security by implementing appropriate physical, electronic and managerial measures to safeguard and secure your personal data.
UNDERAGE USERS

 Coin Dragon does not knowingly collect information on minors using our kiosks, nor is it our intention for underage users to use our services without adult supervision.
CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

 Coin Dragon may change this Policy from time to time. If we make any changes to this Policy, we will change the 'Last Updated' date above, and in some cases, we may provide you with more prominent notice (such as adding a statement to our homepage or sending you an email notification). We encourage you to review the Policy to stay informed about our information practices and the ways you can help protect your privacy.

LANGUAGE

 In case of discrepancies between the English and Chinese versions of this data privacy statement, the English version shall apply and prevail.
